About DIGISOAL

DIGISOAL is the digital arm of MKHULO SOAL HOLDINGS (PTY) LTD — a black-owned, Pretoria-based digital agency that builds and protects businesses online. We do the full stack: register and set up your business to meet South African regulatory requirements, create an elegant, scalable digital ecosystem, and keep it growing with branding, content, and performance marketing. Whether you’re a newly registered SMME, an old-money firm modernising its presence, or a crypto-native project scaling globally, we craft solutions that work — simply, smartly, and with character.
